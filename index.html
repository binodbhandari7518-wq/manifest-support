Manifest — Support

Answers to common questions, and how to reach us if you need more help.

Frequently asked

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore purchase" near the bottom. This re-checks your Apple ID for any prior purchase and unlocks full access automatically — no account or login needed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manifest doesn't process payments directly — subscriptions are managed entirely through your Apple ID. Go to your iPhone's Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Manifest, and cancel from there.

Is my data private?

Yes. Manifest has no account system and no backend server — your intentions, streaks, and journal entries are stored only on your device and never leave it. See our Privacy Policy for details.

My daily reminder isn't showing up.

Check that notifications are enabled for Manifest in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → Manifest, and that a reminder time is set under Settings → Morning Notification inside the app.

Can I use Apple Music or Spotify during my ritual?

Yes — in Settings, under Ritual Music, choose Apple Music or Spotify and pick a playlist. Manifest hands off playback to that app; start your playlist there, then return to Manifest and tap Begin.

The wallpaper I saved isn't showing up in Photos.

Make sure Manifest has permission to add photos — check iPhone Settings → Manifest → Photos is set to "Add Photos Only" or "All Photos". Then try saving again from the wallpaper screen.
